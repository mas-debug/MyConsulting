About Me

Mason Martinelli is currently a student at Miami University pursuing a bachelor's degree in games + simulation. He attended Glenbrook North High School and graduated as a state scholar with a 4.2 GPA. Mason is looking to pursue a career in the field of video game design and production, specifically level design. During his high school years, Mason worked as a lifeguard for the Northbrook Park District from 2021 to 2024. During that time he had a small tenure as an interim pool manager before the pool closed. He was an active member in DECA prior to leaving for college, winning 3rd at regionals for marketing communications. Mason currently lives in Northbrook, Illinois when he is not at Oxford.
